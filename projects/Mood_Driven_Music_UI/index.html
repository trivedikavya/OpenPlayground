How It Works

  • This interface simulates the emotional experience of music using only visual elements.
  • Select a mood to change the visual rhythm, color palette, and motion patterns.
  • Each mood has a unique visual signature: Calm features gentle waves and cool colors, Focus uses rhythmic patterns and green tones, Energy creates vibrant bursts and warm colors.
  • Adjust the sliders to customize tempo, intensity, and color to match your preference.
  • The interface is designed to be fully accessible with reduced motion and high contrast options.
